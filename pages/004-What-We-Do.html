Even the most successful organizations can improve to achieve greater customer service and business effectiveness. We call this Performance Excellence. It drives everything we do.

Northwest Center for Performance Excellence (NWCPE) was created to help businesses achieve world class benchmarks for customer satisfaction, loyalty, and return on investment. We help companies by providing Workshops, Certification Programs, Consulting, and Coaching, all of which can be customized to meet a specific individual or organization’s needs.

The foundation of our methodology is Strategy, Culture, and People. Upon that, we build a system of Continual Improvement, Customer focus, Process, and Analysis.

NWCPE’s partners are the leaders in their respective areas of expertise. Our collaboration with respected organizations of higher learning like Portland State University (Portland, OR), Chemeketa Center for Business and Industry (Salem OR) and the Universidad de San Francisco de Quito (Quito, Ecuador) offer our customers the assurance of value and international recognition for certifications earned through NWCPE. Training and certification programs are available in English and Spanish.
