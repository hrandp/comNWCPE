Process design, execution, and continual improvement consulting to transform your organization’s success

Process Excellence through cutting-edge methodologies including Lean, Six Sigma, Statistical Process Control, and Strategy Development and Deployment. Our consulting services transform your process with customized in-house training and one-on-one coaching

Effective consulting and support from NWCPE takes on many forms but is always based on your organization’s goals and objectives, history, culture, and level of process maturity. Our support can be ad hoc or a complete and comprehensive approach to transformational change and continual improvement for your organization with in-house training, consulting, and one-on-one coaching.

For more than 25 years NWCPE member organizations have supported clients in industries as diverse as plastics molding, food and beverage, high-tech, medical device, custom fabrication shops, wind energy, health service organizations, financial institutions, and governmental public service organizations.

Examples of our services include:

Process Maturity Level auditing, support, and certification.

Voice of the customer survey design and analysis.

Strategy development, deployment, and management.

Quality function deployment to ascertain true customer needs.

Design of Six Sigma Quality Planning for new product development.

Lean manufacturing, service, and office process development and management.

Six Sigma and Lean program development and management.

Statistical Process Control systems design and implementation.

Statistically based process optimization with the application of statistically designed experiments and process design and control development.

Turn-key manufacturing and transactional processes and project management value stream process optimization including: process design, process and quality control, and product preservation from release to manufacturing to delivery and commissioning.
