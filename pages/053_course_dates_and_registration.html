Lean and Six Sigma Course Dates and Registration

Below are the current Lean and Six Sigma Certification Course, dates, and prices. To register Now for courses, click the “Register Now Now” button to open the PSU course page. To continue register Nowing for additional courses, return to the NWCPE site and repeat the process for the next course.

NWCPE’s open-registration courses are designed to assure value — value for you as an individual contributor and value for your organization whether you are just learning about the world of continual process improvement of Lean and Six Sigma or are already a certified practitioner in Lean, Six Sigma, or related disciplines.

You will find workshops to introduce you to the world of continual improvement and give you the background and perspective to make the best decisions for you or your organization’s paths to process excellence. The courses teach and reinforce the fundamental concepts and application of basic tools essential to creating the most successful processes. NWCPE teaches workshops with a varied approach so students get to experience the methodologies through hearing, seeing, and doing. We find it creates the most successful learning environments.
