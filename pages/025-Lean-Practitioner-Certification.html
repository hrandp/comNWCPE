Learning to lead Lean improvement projects

Course Description

The Lean Practitioner Certification one-day workshop leads to certification as a Lean Practitioner. At the conclusion of this program, students will know how to lead a Lean improvement project in their own organization, understand the importance of culture in Lean success, and have gained an esteemed career credential recognized across all industries. The class can be taken individually or as part of a series.
