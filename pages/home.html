Certifications

Certifications in Lean, Six Sigma, Lean Six Sigma, and Statistical Process Control are available.

Six Sigma Yellow Belt Certification Requirements:

  • Completion of Six Sigma Yellow Belt designated workshops
  • Completion of a Yellow Belt qualification exam
  • Completion of project incorporating methodologies introduced in the Yellow Belt curriculum

Six Sigma Green Belt Certification Requirements

  • Completion of Six Sigma Green Belt designated workshops
  • Completion of the Six Sigma Green Belt exam
  • Completion of a project incorporating methodologies introduced in the Green Belt curriculum

Six Sigma Black Belt Certification:

  • Completion of Six Sigma Black Belt designated workshops
  • Completion of the Six Sigma Black Belt exam
  • Completion of a project incorporating methodologies learned in the Six Sigma Black Belt curriculum

Statistical Process Control (SPC) Professional Certification:

  • Completion of SPC designated workshops
  • Completion of the SPC exam
  • Completion of a project incorporating methodologies learned in the SPC curriculum
